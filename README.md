## Dashboard Components

* Total Revenue: SAR 406,873

* Total Orders: 150

* Average Revenue per Product: SAR 2,712

* Top 3 Products by Revenue: Products D, B, and A

* Revenue by Region: Comparison across Saudi cities, with Jeddah as the top-performing region

* Monthly Sales Trend: Line chart showing daily revenue fluctuations and peak sales days

## Key Insights

* Strong overall sales performance driven by a small set of high-performing products

* Noticeable variation in revenue across regions, highlighting regional demand differences

* Sales trends fluctuate throughout the month, indicating peak and low activity periods

## Recommendations

* Focus marketing and inventory efforts on top-performing products (Products D, B, and A) to maximize revenue.

* Replicate successful sales strategies from high-performing regions such as Jeddah in lower-performing cities.

* Monitor daily sales trends to identify peak periods and optimize promotions or staffing accordingly.
